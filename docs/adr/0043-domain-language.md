# 0043 - Domain Vocabulary

## Status

Accepted

## Context

Errors should sound like physics lab guidance, not parser internals.

## Decision

Use words such as marker, track, sample, time, position, confidence, gap, outlier,
fit, acceleration, period, and friction coefficient proxy.

## Consequences

Messages are easier for students and educators to act on.

## Alternatives Considered

Developer-centric names such as selector, parse token, or undefined field were
rejected.
